Armour R&D Project Aims to Reduce Energy Use to Heat Alumni Hall.

Afshin Faramarzi, a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering, was awarded the 2018-2019 Jende Augustine Hsu Scholarship. The Jende Augustine Hsu Scholarship was established in 2017 with a generous gift from Yen Der (Robert) Tsao, with the goal of helping to provide a graduate education in civil engineering to those for whom it might not otherwise be possible.
